Who we are

About TutorConnect

TutorConnect helps students find reliable tutors for academic support and exam preparation. We connect learners with qualified tutors in various subjects, ensuring personalised learning experiences and improved academic performance.

Our mission is to empower students to achieve their academic goals by providing access to experienced tutors who can guide them through their learning journey. We believe every student has the potential to excel with the right support.

We focus on creating a safe, supportive, and engaging learning environment where students can build confidence, strengthen their understanding of key concepts, and develop essential study skills.

Through innovative technology and dedicated educators, TutorConnect bridges the gap between students and expert tutors, offering flexible online learning, personalised tutoring plans, and progress tracking tailored to each student's needs.

    Terms of Service

    Last updated: 1 January 2025

    1. Using TutorConnect

    TutorConnect connects students with independent tutors for academic support. By creating an account you confirm that the information you provide is accurate and that you are old enough, or have guardian permission, to use the service.

    2. Booking sessions

    When you book a session you agree to attend at the scheduled time. Tutors set their own availability and rates, which are shown before you confirm. Cancellations made at least 24 hours in advance are eligible for rescheduling.

    3. Conduct

    We expect respectful, honest communication between students and tutors. Harassment, abuse, or attempts to arrange payment outside the platform may result in account suspension.

    4. Payments & refunds

    Session fees are processed securely. If a tutor fails to attend a confirmed session, you are entitled to a full refund or a free rebooking.

    5. Changes

    We may update these terms from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated by email or an in-app notice. This is a student project demonstration and not a binding legal agreement.
